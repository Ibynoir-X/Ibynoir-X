## ğŸ›¡ What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of **protecting systems, networks, programs, and data** from unauthorized access, theft, damage, or disruption.  
It ensures **confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA)** of digital assets.

Key domains include:
- **Network Security:** Firewalls, IDS/IPS, VPNs, Wireshark, Nmap.  
- **Application Security:** OWASP Top 10, secure coding, vulnerability scanning.  
- **Cloud Security:** AWS, Azure, GCP security, IAM, and monitoring.  
- **Information Security:** Encryption, hashing, authentication, policies.  
- **Endpoint Security:** Antivirus, EDR, patch management.  
- **Incident Response:** Detection, investigation, mitigation, forensic analysis.  
- **Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing:** Legal hacking to find and fix vulnerabilities.  
- **Cyber Threat Intelligence:** Monitoring emerging threats and malware trends.
